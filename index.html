About

We have been selling apples in MA for over thirty years. Little Big Apple is a family owned business based in Quincy MA. We grow our own apples and sell them fresh and in baked goods.

We have a 12 acre orchard right on the edge of Quincy.  We have 13 different type of apples to choose from, or you can get our variety pack which contains all 13. 

 We have a team of 25 people.  If curious about joining us, you can find an application below.  Jobs include baking, picking apples, and  sorting apples.

About our Apples

We offer 13 types of apples, ranging from big and sweet to small and tart. We also offer baked goods made with fresh apples.

ABOUT

At Little Big Apple, we are devoted to bringing you the best of the best when it comes to apples. We source the highest quality apples from local orchards and farmers to ensure that you get the freshest and most delicious apples possible. We also take pride in providing a variety of apples to suit different tastes and preferences. Whether you’re looking for sweet, tart, or something in between, you’ll find it in our selection. Visit our store today to discover the perfect apple for you.

LOCATION

We are currently located in Quincy MA. We plan on expanding our business to several locations in the local Boston Area.
